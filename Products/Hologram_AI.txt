Hologram AI — Product Concept & Content Pack   
Elevator Pitch 
Hologram AI is a real‑time telepresence system that projects a life‑size, 3D holographic 
presence of a remote person into your room—so conversations feel like sitting face‑to‑face. 
No video tiles. Natural eye‑contact, spatial audio, and body language—restoring confidence, 
comfort, and clear communication. 
Problem 
Video calls flatten humans into 2D squares, reducing presence and non‑verbal cues. In 
high‑trust conversations, this creates discomfort, misunderstanding, and fatigue. 
Solution & How It Works 
Volumetric capture (depth + RGB + pose) → neural compression → low‑latency transport → 
life‑size holographic rendering with spatial audio. People perceive each other across the 
table, not through a screen. 
Capture Options: 
• Single‑camera depth rig (MVP). 
• Multi‑camera volumetric booth (enterprise). 
• Mobile LiDAR capture where available. 
 
Render Options: 
• Light‑field/pepper‑scrim kiosk. 
• AR/spatial glasses for portable setups. 
• Transparent OLED/holographic fans (partial body). 
 
Core Stack: 
• AI vision (depth completion, segmentation, relighting, NeRF/diffusion view synthesis). 
• Low‑latency transport (WebRTC/QUIC), edge compute, neural codecs. 
• Spatial audio, echo cancellation, lip‑sync, gaze correction. 
• Security & privacy: encryption, access control, audit logs. 
Key Features 
• One‑tap connect; life‑size projection; true eye‑line. 
• Spatial audio & beam‑forming mics. 
• Gesture/expression fidelity; relighting; background separation. 
• Recording & replay; watermarking; consent prompts. 
• Admin controls; enterprise security (SSO/SCIM). 
Use Cases 
Leadership townhalls, enterprise sales, tele‑education, telemedicine, hiring panels, 
family/life events. 
Product Tiers 
V1 (MVP): torso‑up hologram, 20–30 fps, kiosk projection, India beta. 
V2 (Pro): multi‑camera capture, full‑body, occlusion handling, cloud+edge orchestration. 
V3 (Enterprise): multi‑site mesh, recording, compliance packs, APIs/SDKs. 
Privacy, Safety & Ethics 
Opt‑in consent, encryption, secure deletion, watermarking, accessibility 
(captions/translation/audio description). 
Success Outcomes 
2–3× higher comfort & trust vs. video calls; faster decisions; improved sales close‑rates & 
learning outcomes.
